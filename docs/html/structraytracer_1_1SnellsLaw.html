Provide gradientCalculator calculate the plane of rarefaction. Provide collisionalFrequencyCalculator to evaluate the index of rarefaction. Density, temperature and ionization values are expected to change.

Function to be used in Laser::generateIntersections() as findDirection.

Finds new direction based on Snells law between the two Element, density (provides an index of rarefaction) and gradient (provides edge direction). Could throw error no intersection found!
